What is DeFi?

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) refers to a set of financial applications that run on blockchain technology. DeFi protocols provide users with financial services such as lending, borrowing, and trading, without the need for intermediaries such as banks. DeFi applications are built on decentralized networks, which means that they are transparent, secure, and accessible to anyone with an internet connection.

DeFi tokens are at the heart of the DeFi ecosystem. These tokens are used to incentivize users to participate in the network, and they are also used to pay transaction fees and facilitate trading on decentralized exchanges.
